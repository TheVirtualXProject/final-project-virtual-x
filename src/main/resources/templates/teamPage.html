Meet the Developers



Welcome to Virtual X, hope you had fun visiting our Social, Remotely website!

It was just an idea, we built the Virtual X website with the knowledge we learned from boot camp and additional research, plus a lot of Googling and Stack Overflowing, we were able to pull this together under 3 weeks. Through this project, we learned to better communicate, to listen, to share, to speak up, to be wrong, to be right,to contribute, to be frustrated, to be joyful...

The most important thing we learned is, to be A TEAM!

Bring a friend, come back and play anytime, the site is OPEN for everyone at this time, just enter the link http://thevirtualx.com.

It was our pleasure to "Meet" you! Thank you for visiting!
