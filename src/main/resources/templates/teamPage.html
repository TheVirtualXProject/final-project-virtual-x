Welcome to Virtual X, hope you had fun visiting our Social, Remotely website!

It was just an idea, we built the Virtual X website with the knowledge we learned from boot camp and additional research, plus a lot of Googling and Stack Overflowing, we were able to pull this together under 3 weeks.
